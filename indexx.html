Welcome to ALPHA Tech Academy


Alpha Tech Academy is a Learning and Development Academy, Headquartered in Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India. ATAS was founded in 2015. The company is promoted and managed by a group of professionals. Our primary focus is to transform and empower fresh college graduates and existing employees of corporates with the right Technical, Communication, and Leadership acumen to make them employable and Industry ready.


Our aim is to enhance essential employability skills among fresh college graduates by providing industry relevant skills to ensure the students are employed. We work with various campuses to improve the quality of technical education by providing an Integrated learning Program at Campuses and Industry Connect Program for better Campus placements. And to be the right source for corporates for Staffing of Fresh College Graduates and Lateral Training of IT employees Re-Skilling, Up-Skilling & on Cross-Skilling.

Our Values


MISSION:ATAS is committed to delivering high-quality training by giving learners a fulfilling experience. We help apprentices realise their potential and achieve their life-long aspirations. We promote and support employee development and organisational effectiveness through the provision of high-quality, educational training programmes.


VISION: "Our vision is to become a beacon of excellence in education, empowering individuals to realize their full potential and thrive in a rapidly evolving world. Through innovative teaching methods, personalized mentorship, and a commitment to lifelong learning, we aim to inspire a culture of continuous growth and achievement. Our institute seeks to nurture intellectual curiosity, cultivate critical thinking skills, and foster a spirit of collaboration and innovation. By equipping our students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to succeed, we aspire to be a catalyst for positive change in society, shaping future leaders who will make meaningful contributions to their professions and communities."
